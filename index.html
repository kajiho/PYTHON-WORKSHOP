FACILITIES
Nepalgunj Campus of Management of Technology provides a range of facilities to support students' academic and extracurricular activities. The campus features modern classrooms equipped with multimedia tools, state-of-the-art computer labs with high-speed internet, and a well-stocked library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. Additionally, there are spacious seminar halls for workshops and guest lectures, various sports and recreational facilities to promote physical fitness, and campus-wide high-speed Wi-Fi. Dedicated counseling and support services are also available to assist students in their academic and personal growth, creating a conducive environment for holistic development.
